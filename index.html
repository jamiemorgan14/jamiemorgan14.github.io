my story

Born and raised in Olympia, Washington, I developed a love of rainy days, the Seahawks, and sitting inside playing on my computer during those rainy days. A baseball player through college, I learned the importance of teamwork and leadership. After working as a cook in a local restaurant and deciding my next step, I decided to join the Air Force and became an F-15 mechanic. I developed countless problem-solving skills in my career and furthered the leadership qualities I learned earlier in life. After 6 years of the military, I became more interested in the computers I played with when I was younger, and started learning to code. I spent months teaching myself, but didn’t make the progress I felt I was capable of. I enrolled in Boise CodeWorks in order to learn from quality instructors and work on real-life projects with people rather than just struggling my way through learning on my own. Since starting CodeWorks I’ve refined my skills and learned what really goes in to developing and creating websites and web applications. I’m loving my life, loving what I do, and I’m excited about continuing my journey as a web developer and programmer. I’m currently searching for a company that I can bring value to, so if you think I’m your guy read on and reach out! Jamie Morgan
